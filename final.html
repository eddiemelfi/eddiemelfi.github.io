Final Project

For our final project, my partern, Nick, and I made a parody of the 2013 hit mobile app, Flappy Bird. It's been taken off the app stores so we decided to make one ourselves with our bitmojis!
To use the program, you start off with the start screen. Use your mouse and click begin. Once you hit begin, the game will start. Using the spacebar, press and hold it down to make our bitmoji jump and release the spacebar to make the bitmoji fall.
Avoiding the green pipes, try to make it to the end of the game! If you hit a pipe then the game over screen will pop up. Thanks for playing and enjoy!
